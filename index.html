Purpose:

DevFromZero was created to prove that valuable skills don't require expensive courses or formal education.
With determination and the right mindset, anyone can learn and grow using the freely available resources around them.

This journey is about starting from zero- learning by doing, building with purpose and growing step by step into a skilled developer.

About me:

I'm someone who started with no background in coding: Just curiosity and the drive to learn

DevFromZero is my way of proving that anyone can grow into a developer using free tools, dedication and consistency.

I'm not an expert; yet. But I'm learning everyday, building real, projects with what I have learned, and getting better with each step.

Motive:

Learn Daily
I dedicate time everyday to learn new skills, starting from HTML and moving towards Full Stack Development.
Build Projects
Every concept I learn will be applied in small or real-world projects, which will be added to Projects Made
Update Regularly
